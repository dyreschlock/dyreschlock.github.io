Impressions....

I got the greatest (dumbest) thing in the mail today!

It's a carrying case for the Nintendo DS shaped like a little doggie! There's a zipper that goes along his mouth, where you can store your Nintendo DS.

There's another pouch underneath that one where you can store DS games, the DS charger, and whatever else.

You can also store your hand inside the alternate pouch and reach around the mouth so you can have your little doggie speak to you.

The reason I got this thing is because it's totally awesome, and it was free with my purchase of a Nintendo 3DS from Amazon. They also gave me $25 credit towards a 3DS game.

Here's the link if you want one for yourself!
