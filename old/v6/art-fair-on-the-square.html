Art Fair on the Square

Art Fair on the Square is an annual event that happens on Capitol Square in Madison. Local artists bring prints and works to their booths, while everyone walks around and browses. Each year, I usually found something interesting to put on my walls.

Note: This post was made in Mar 2025 and back-dated to the time of the events
