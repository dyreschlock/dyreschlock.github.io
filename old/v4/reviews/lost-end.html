Lost End

I love this show.

Well, my predictions in yesterday's post were quite a ways off, and my expectations were exceeded in ways I didn't even think of. It feels rather weird though.. this whole Season kinda felt like not much has happened (up until the last 4 episodes), but the show has come a long way since last year. We know many of the Others, have seen all the Dharma stations, and know what happened to the Dharma Initiative. We also know who's been getting busy with each other on the Island, and Locke has become a badass again.

And what the hell is up with Mikhail.. he gets the shit kicked out of him every single episode he's on, and has even died twice, but still comes out okay the next episode. I think the writers took a page out of South Park for this guy. Maybe the name Mikhail is the Russian equivalent of Kenny.

Anywho, Now that all of my TV shows have completed until Fall, it's time to go outside and frolic in the bask of Summer. Or (something i'll probably do) watch all the episodes of Lost again from the very beginning by showing them to new people.

In the News

Season 4: Feb 2008 - May 2008
Season 5: Feb 2009 - May 2009
Season 6: Feb 2010 - May 2010

Also: I will buy these once they have all eight stations included. They are missing the looking glass, and the doors.
