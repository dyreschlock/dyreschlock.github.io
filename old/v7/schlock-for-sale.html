Schlock for Sale

Posted in Others, Updates | August 19, 2007 Comments Off

This weekend, I headed home to gather large amounts of possessions in a effort to rid myself of them. Throughout the years, I've accumulated quite a bit of stuff, much of which was a completely unnecessary purchase, or something I bought "spur of the moment", or any other random excuses you can think of.

I think it's time for my collection to go through a purge to trim away the fat/useless items and increase the quality of my collection and give way for new items as they should come.

Here's the big 'ol list: http://theschlock.com/photo/070818_stuff/schlock_for_sale.txt

Most of it is Anime VHS, but there's plenty of other stuff on there too. I still need to add the VHS that Anna from work dumped on me.
