Cards of Legend
Thoughts

I got a package in the mail today from Fangamer. A couple of weeks ago they listed a set of Legend of Zelda playing cards that looked rather pretty. They were created by Internet user, Nelde. You can find their artwork on deviant art right here : http://nelde.deviantart.com/. The set is now available from Fangamer, and you can buy one for $15, if you like : http://www.fangamer.net/products/cards-of-legend.

You can find plenty of pictures of the set on that site, but I took a couple pictures of my own.

There are pretty. But then again, how many decks of cards do you need? Plus, I only bought a single deck, so it wouldn't work very well for multiple deck games.
