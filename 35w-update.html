35W Update

Sunday November 25, 2007 Jim Hendricks 35w disaster // Miscellanous .America

While in Minneapolis this weekend, I made sure to stop off at 35W to see how the progress of cleaning up the old 35W and building a new 35W was going. On September 23rd, the last time I was in Minneapolis, I took a couple pictures which told a much different story than the ones today.

Here's the previous entry: http://www.theschlock.com/blog/?p=368

Today, all the wreckage is gone, and the surface is set for the construction of a new bridge.
