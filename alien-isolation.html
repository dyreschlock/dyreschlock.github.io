Alien Isolation

Thursday February 11, 2016 Jim Hendricks alien-isolation playstation screenshots video-games game-reviews reviews // Games/Game Thoughts

Alien Isolation is perhaps the best game to come from the Alien franchise. It follows the events of the first alien movie as your portray Ellen Ripley's daughter searching the remnants of her mother. The Nostromo flight recorder from its demise has been recovered and brought to a space station called Sevastopol.

You travel there and wade through a mess of lethal corporate androids and mercenaries while being stalked being an alien.

Besides creating some amazing tension moving through scenes, Alien Isolation does its best to recreate much of the iconic sets and scenery from the original movie.
