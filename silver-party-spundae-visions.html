Christopher Lawrence, "Silver Party" @ Visions, Spundae, Chicago

Saturday May 17, 2003 Jim Hendricks christopher-lawrence visions chicago-club // Music .Events

Update on March 22nd, 2014

These are photos from the Silver Party held at Visions / Spundae in Chicago on May 17th, 2003 headlined by Christopher Lawrence.

As the trance scene continue to explode in the US, it seemed like all the major players were from England. But then there was Christopher Lawrence, an American. I went to Visions to hear him and see what the difference might have been between the rich European trance sound, and an American making his own in the scene.

Overall, he didn't really leave an impressions on me. I don't really remember having that great of a time that night for whatever reason.
