what a spectacular weekend we had at the Quest listening to the gods of trance.. it was packed the likes of which i've never seen before ... but it was full of very many, many, many negative atributes.. such is the price to see Sasha and Digweed though...

negative experience #1 : it was crowded... and like i've already said.. its never been this crowded... you could hardly move in the crowd and when someone did, they usually forced their way through.. violently.. pushing and shoving was amuck and it was something i wasn't very keen to...

negative experience #2 ... because it was crowded.. and everyone was moving ... the heat level rose quite high ... and there was every kind of man stink and woman stink in the air .. ack ... after being on the dance floor for a total of 3 some hours.. i could take no more .. and once you left the dance floor.. you ain't getting back...

negative experience #3.... " the talent does not want their picture taken... if you are caught with a camera inside.. you will be removed along with your camera... and your film will also be removed... " ... nothing was worse than this ... son of a mother fucker... this pissed all of us off...

negative experience #4... some of those lights were pointing at the exact wrong place... if not being able to take pictures was bad enough ... most of the lighting effects we went to highlight the crowd so that Sasha and Digweed could take clear pictures of us ... and these weren't just ambient lights... this was like staring into the high beams of car coming straight at you around 11 o'clock at night ... very bad for the retinas.. i wish i would have broughten in my blue specs.. oh well ....

negative experience #5.. once i get back to school i have ungodly amounts of homework due the exact next day ... i had a program to finish, a paper to write, a friend who needed C++ tutoring, and then spamm to attend ... which is the rason you are getting this update now instead of monday night... this wednesday morning is the first spat of free time i have ... phew...

anywho... such is the price to pay to fulfill a life long dream... the whole expexience is still quite on the positive end of things ... Sasha and Digweed truly are the gods of trance.. and i salute thee... i would have bought a Delta Heavy Tshirt but i didn't want to spend $35 on something that didn't even have Sasha or Digweed printed on it ^_^ ...
